All Black! Be quiet! PURE BASE 500 Build

Foreword

I didn’t waste time though I was resting at home. So I helped build a system. After trying out builds with all kinds of lighting effects, a system with out lighting effects seems to be more out-standing. I used Ryzen 7 3700X + X570 + RTX 2080 platform. Considering that it has no lighting effects, I chose be quiet!‘s PURE BASE 500 for the case. It has a simple but elegant design and is pretty easy to install. For cooling, an air-cooling solution with out RGB was the best choice. It turned out to be extreamly quiet. And as for the power-supply, I used a HCG750 with 10 years waranty.

Here are the basic requirements for this build:

  • Powerful and can handle jobs for designing, video and photo editing and can provide a smooth experience playing PUBG or watching hi-res videos.
  • Look simple and does need to have any lighting effects.
  • Have good expandability and is easy to add additional memory and storage later.
